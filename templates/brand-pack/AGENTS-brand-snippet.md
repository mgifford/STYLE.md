## Brand consistency guardrails

- Treat `STYLES.md` as the brand source of truth for voice, tokens, and structure.
- Reuse approved components and class contracts before introducing new markup.
- Use existing CSS custom properties; do not add raw hex colors in page markup.
- Keep sentence case headings and authoritative-peer voice.
- For new components, add a contract entry in `brand-pack/components.md` before use.
- Never override `ACCESSIBILITY.md` or `SUSTAINABILITY.md` constraints.
